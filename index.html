Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to VisionIQ ("we", "our", "us"), an AI-powered image analysis and intelligent assistant app developed by DevTech Solution. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully. By using VisionIQ, you agree to the practices described herein.

2. Information We Collect

a) Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials via Firebase Authentication. If you choose not to create an account, we may assign an anonymous identifier through Firebase Anonymous Authentication.

b) User-Generated Content

  • Images: Photos you upload or capture for AI analysis are processed to provide you with results. Images are sent to our secure servers for analysis and are not stored permanently unless you explicitly save a chat.
  • Chat Messages: Conversations you have with the AI assistant, including text and any attached files.
  • Saved Chats: Chat histories you choose to save are stored in your account.
  • Memories: Personal facts, preferences, or instructions you provide to personalize the AI assistant, stored in Firebase Firestore under your account.
  • Custom System Prompts: Any custom persona or instructions you configure are stored locally on your device.

c) Audio Data

If you use voice input features, audio is temporarily processed for transcription and is not stored after transcription is complete.

d) Usage Data

We may collect non-personal usage analytics such as app version, device type, operating system, crash logs, and general usage patterns to improve the App.

e) Subscription Information

If you subscribe to a premium plan, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your payment information directly.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the App's features and AI analysis capabilities.
  • To authenticate your identity and manage your account.
  • To personalize your experience using your saved memories and preferences.
  • To process your images and queries through AI models and return results.
  • To generate images or content you request.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app stability.
  • To communicate important updates about the App or your account.

4. Third-Party Services

VisionIQ uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, Cloud Functions, Firestore database, and Remote Config. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI: AI processing for text, image analysis, image generation, and audio transcription. Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini: Alternative AI processing provider. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and in-app purchase management. Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Your data is transmitted to these services securely through our backend Cloud Functions. We do not share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored using Firebase services hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure.
  • API keys and sensitive credentials are stored in Google Cloud Secret Manager and are never exposed on the client side.
  • All communication between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, but no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active.
  • Saved Chats & Memories: Retained until you delete them or delete your account.
  • Images: Processed in real-time and not permanently stored on our servers unless part of a saved chat.
  • Audio: Temporarily processed for transcription and immediately discarded.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data and account.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: You may stop using the App at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

8. Children's Privacy

VisionIQ is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Your continued use of VisionIQ after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
